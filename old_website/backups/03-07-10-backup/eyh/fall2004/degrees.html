Degrees of a Circle

Every circle has 360 degrees. So, to have Logo turn all the way around, and end up facing the same way he started, you type goto turnright, degrees = 360 or goto turnleft, degrees = 360. But this will make Logo very dizzy. To have him turn less than completely around, use a number that is less than 360.

The picture below shows you the instructions you should use to make Logo turn around. Remember, the instructions are relative to the way Logo is currently facing. So, if Logo is facing toward the top of the screen, then the instruction goto turnleft, degrees = 90 will tell Logo to face left. But if Logo is already facing to the left, that instruction will tell Logo to turn so that he is facing the bottom of the screen. If you want Logo to turn less than 90 degrees but more than 45 degrees just use a number in between 45 and 90.
