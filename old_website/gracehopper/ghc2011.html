Grace Hopper 2011

The Grace Hopper Celebration of Women in Computing 2011 was held in Portland, Oregon, from November 9-12.

The Anita Borg Institute (ABI) has volunteers sign up to take notes for various sessions at GHC. These notes are available here. The ABI YouTUbe Channel also has a variety of videos of conference events. In particular, we highly recommend watching Facebook COO Sheryl Sandberg's keynote.

Several WACM women took notes at conference events; you can read about Lena and Emily's experiences.

We also have some slides about general GHC information here (note that some information is specific for GHC 2012).
